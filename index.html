Bubble Tea

My favorite beverage

Bubble tea, or boba tea, is a tea-based drink that originated in Taiwan in the early 1980s. It has many varities and flavors. Bubble tea most commonly consists of tea accompanied by chewy tapioca balls ("boba"), but it can be made with other toppings such as grass jelly, aloe vera, red bean, and popping boba.
